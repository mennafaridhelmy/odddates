WHY CHOOSING ODD DATES?

Since pharaonic times, Siwa Oasis has hosted farms producing some of the most prized dates in Egypt. In that time, Siwa’s farmers have perfected the art of growing tall, spiny date palm trees in scorching heat.

Western Sahara has a very large reservoir of groundwater.
Weather conditions of high temperature and lack of relative humidity help to produce varieties of half dry dates.
Deep sandy soil and good drainage make it easy to reclaim.
